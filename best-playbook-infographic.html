Discovery and Delivery Playbook

How we take work from idea to monitoring
Key principle Sign-off is targeted but not a blocker. Once the team has enough understanding, story creation runs in parallel with finalising sign-off.
Per-release flow · 9 phases
1
Discovery
Liza and Christine align on requirements through calls, mockups, whiteboards, and shared sheets. Aim for a requirements meeting and stakeholder sign-off.
/best-mockup
2
Spike + Story creation
Spikes and user stories created and refined. Fix version defined. RKD created in Confluence and updated continuously.
/create-spike /create-user-story /release-kickoff /update-rkd
3
Estimation
Release owner runs an estimation meeting using a shared sheet to collect estimates from team members.
/estimation
4
Test scenarios
E2E scenarios written in parallel with engineering. Output goes to a new tab in the BT E2E Master List sheet.
/create-test-scenarios /regression-tests
5
Pre-E2E (two parts)
Two internal walkthroughs before any stakeholder is involved.
5a — With engineering team: internal walkthrough of test scenarios.
5b — With PM (Christine): review before exposing to stakeholders.
no skill
6
UAT preparation
UAT Confluence page prepared from the test sheet (UAT-flagged rows only), rewritten for stakeholders without backend technical detail.
/create-uat
7
E2E with stakeholder
Stakeholder-facing E2E session walking through the feature live. UAT page is then handed over for them to execute and sign off.
no skill
8
Release
After UAT sign-offs, release goes to production with release notes sent ahead. Internal and stakeholder Slack announcements drafted.
/create-release-notes /release-announcement
9
Post-release monitoring
Feature monitored on production with a report and SLO tracking performance. Bugs raised by stakeholders flow into the BAU PSR triage process.
no skill
BAU · daily & ongoing
Runs independently of any release.
/triage-psrDaily
Triage all stakeholder-raised PSR tickets. Entry point for any new bug or issue raised against BEST.
/create-psrAd-hoc
Log new PSR bug tickets, with templates for known recurring bugs.
/bvaDaily 17:00 VN
BVA team summary draft posted to #best-trials-and-alex.
/create-showcaseMonthly
Customer Operations Showcase deck refresh for SLT / CEO audience.
Things to remember
1
Sign-off is not a gate
Discovery aims for sign-off, but story creation can start as soon as the team understands the work.
2
RKD ties it together
The Release Kickoff Document is the per-release artefact spanning phases 2 → 9 for one fix version.
3
Pre-E2E is two parts
First with engineering, then with PM Christine — before any stakeholder sees the work.
4
BAU runs in parallel
PSR triage, bug logging, BVA, and the monthly showcase are independent of any release flow.
