Things to remember

↻
RKD ties it together
Per-release artefact spanning phases 2 → 14 for one fix version.
2
Pre-E2E is two parts
First with engineering, then with the Product Team before any stakeholder sees it.
→
Release notes go before deploy
Product Team drafts during UAT and sends to stakeholders before full release so teams can be briefed.
∞
BAU runs in parallel
PSR triage, bug logging, and the monthly showcase are independent of any release.
